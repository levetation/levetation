Burnt House Farm, Backwell, BS48 4AD

Coming south from Bristol direction:

A370 to Backwell. Pass Rising Sun Pub and watch out for turning on right with stone cattle trough on the corner. Turn right into Chelvey Road and the workshop is in farm buildings through the gate (marked "Burnt House Farm") 300 yards along on the right hand side.

Coming north from Weston super Mare direction:

A370 to Backwell. Just after the 30mph signs at Backwell, turn left into Chelvey Road (stone cattle trough on the corner). The workshop is in farm buildings through the gate (marked "Burnt House Farm") 300 yards along on the right hand side.
